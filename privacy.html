Thriftique values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, or mailing address, provided when you make a purchase, join our loyalty program, or sign up for events or newsletters.
  • Payment Information: Collected securely through our payment processor for purchases.
  • Usage Information: Such as website visits or social media interactions to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process transactions and fulfill orders.
  • Communicate updates about promotions, events, or new arrivals.
  • Improve customer service and enhance your shopping experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in payment processing, marketing, or logistics.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in emails or reaching out to us.
