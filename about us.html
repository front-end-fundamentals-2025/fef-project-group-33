Welcome to Thriftique,

Jönköping’s premier destination for sustainable fashion and second-hand treasures! At Thriftique, we believe style doesn’t have to come at the expense of the planet. Our carefully curated collection features high-quality, pre-loved clothing, accessories, and home goods, all at affordable prices. By giving second-hand items a new life, we help our community embrace eco-friendly shopping while making fashion accessible to everyone.

Originally located in the heart of Jönköping, now fully online, we’re proud to combine sustainability with style, creating a shopping experience that’s as rewarding for you as it is for the environment. Visit us to find your next unique piece and join the movement for a greener, more stylish future!
