What is Thriftique Premium?

Thriftique Premium is our exclusive membership program offering special perks like free shipping on eligible orders, early access to sales, and personalized shopping recommendations. Join Thriftique Premium to elevate your shopping experience.

What is the status of my order?

You can check your order status by logging into your Thriftique account and going to the "My Orders" section. There, you’ll see real-time updates on your order’s progress, including shipment tracking information.

How to contact a seller?

To contact a seller, go to the product listing and click on the "Contact Seller" button. You can also reach out from your order details page if you’ve already made a purchase. Sellers usually respond within 1–2 business days.

How to get help with an order?

If you need assistance with an order, visit the "Contact" section, and contact Thriftique’s customer service team.

How to search for items on thriftique?

Use the search bar at the top of the homepage to find specific items.
